Detailed Product Grades

Select the ideal specification for your formulation.

Natural Brown

Overview

Natural Brown Cocoa Powder gives a classic cocoa note and balanced color profile for standard bakery and beverage production.

Technical Profile

  • Fat content: 10% - 12%
  • pH range: 5.1 - 6.0
  • Color profile: Natural brown
  • Application fit: Bakery, beverage, confectionery fillings

Why This Grade

Suitable for formulations that need authentic cocoa flavor with stable process behavior across repeated batches.

Alkalized Brown

Overview

Alkalized Brown Cocoa Powder delivers smoother flavor and improved dispersion for beverage and bakery applications.

Technical Profile

  • Fat content: 10% - 12%
  • pH range: 6.5 - 6.8
  • Color profile: Brown
  • Application fit: Instant drinks, bakery premix, chocolate compounds

Why This Grade

Designed for factories that need dependable color and mild alkalized taste for medium-tone finished products.

Dark Brown

Overview

Alkalized Dark Brown Cocoa Powder provides stronger visual depth and richer cocoa expression in baked products.

Technical Profile

  • Fat content: 10% - 12%
  • pH range: 6.7 - 7.5
  • Color profile: Dark brown
  • Application fit: Cookies, cakes, biscuit cream

Why This Grade

Works well for producers targeting darker appearance while maintaining stable mixing and repeatable output.

Very Dark

Overview

Alkalized Very Dark Brown Cocoa Powder is tailored for strong cocoa color impact and consistent lot-to-lot appearance.

Technical Profile

  • Fat content: 10% - 12%
  • pH range: 6.8 - 7.8
  • Color profile: Very dark brown
  • Application fit: Cookies, cakes, fillings, premix

Why This Grade

Helps reduce formulation variance for manufacturers that require high color intensity in scaled production.

Intense Black

Overview

Alkalized Black Cocoa Powder offers the deepest cocoa tone for products that need a near-black finish.

Technical Profile

  • Fat content: 10% - 12%
  • pH range: 7.5 - 7.9
  • Color profile: Black
  • Application fit: Sandwich cookies, black bakery products

Why This Grade

Best for visual-forward formulations where dark appearance consistency is a key quality target.
